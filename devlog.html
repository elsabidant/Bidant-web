2025-11-07

  • Modification of the project: the idea of testing whether anticipatory works ‘predict’ the future better than others would necessarily have yielded significant results due to the amount of data.
  • New idea: test whether, in science fiction films, those that predict the future more accurately are less successful culturally. (Possibility of testing the hypothesis with linear regression).
  • Finalisation of writing and publication of pre-registration.

2025-10-21

  • Changes in operationalization : ask the LLM to extract 5 factual claims per film, then to evaluate independently from the work whether it was true at the time of publication and whether it is true today.
  • Manually annotating and testing the prompts on 10 films.
  • Rewritting of the protocole in the preregistration.

2025-10-12

  • Writing the code to run the prompts.
  • Manually annotating and testing the prompts on 10 films and 10 books.
  • Minor issue with the prompts: reassessing the operationalization.

2025-10-05

  • Writing the Pre Registration.
  • Writing some LLM prompts to test our hypothesis.
  • Next step: prompt testing.

2025-09-28

  • Wikidata harvesting attempts: mixed results due to timeouts and missing data (publication dates, authors).
  • Meeting with Edgar Dubourg: discussed methodology and article outline ideas.
  • Next step: write the study protocol and draft a pre-registration.
