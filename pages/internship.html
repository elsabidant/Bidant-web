Part 1

January to June 2025

Objectives

Understand the interactions between cognitive mechanisms, personality traits and socio-demographic characteristics in the smoking cessation intention.

Question

Which cognitive mechanisms are associated with smoking cessation intention, and how does this differ depending on smokers’ personality profiles and socio-demographic characteristics ?

Hypothesis

Hypothesis 1

Smokers with different personalities (dimensions and facets) will be differently influenced by cognitive ingredients in smoking cessation intention.

Hypothesis 2

The cognitive ingredients that encourage smoking cessation will differ according to smokers’ socio-demographic characteristics.

Hypothesis 3

Smokers with different personalities (dimensions and facets) will be differently influenced by the arguments used in advertising.

Hypothesis 4

The arguments that encourage smoking cessation will differ according to smokers' socio-demographic characteristics.

Analysis methods

  • 1000 regression models with Lasso regularisation to test each hypothesis.
  • Selection of variables that appear more than 50% of the time in the 1000 models.

Results

  • 1000 regression models with Lasso regularisation to test each hypothesis.
  • Selection of variables that appear more than 50% of the time in the 1000 models.

Part 2

November to June 2026

Objectives

Evaluate whether exposure to personalized anti-smoking ads , developed based on the results of the first part of the study, significantly increases the likelihood of attempting to quit smoking compared to exposure to randomly selected anti-smoking ads.

Question

Which cognitive mechanisms are associated with smoking cessation intention, and how does this differ depending on smokers’ personality profiles and socio-demographic characteristics ?

Hypothesis

Hypothesis 1

Smokers who have been exposed to advertising tailored to their personality are more likely to attempt to quit smoking.

Methods

  • Creation of an algorithms to personalise ads based on personality traits, age and socio economic situation.
