Annotation method

We used a Large Language Model (GPT-4) to automatically annotate each film with factual claims about the world it depicts.

Claims extraction
Extract 5 concrete, testable claims about the film’s world.

Past truth

  • Finalisation of writing and publication of pre-registration.
